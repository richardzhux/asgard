# Chunk 13 (units 9841-10740)

**Key Concepts / Sections**

1. **General Waterfowl Regulations Noted**
   - Many WMAs: shooting hours **½ hour before sunrise to 2 p.m.**
   - Many WMAs: **no entry before 4 a.m.**
   - Some areas have **mobility-impaired blinds (quota hunts)**.
   - Restrictions on **permanent vs. temporary blinds**.
   - Multiple **seasonal closure dates** for specific sub-areas.

---

### Individual Areas & Rules

**Twin Ponds Field (unspecified WMA, context: blinds 16 & 17)**
- WMA road **closed during waterfowl season**; stand-by hunters must **enter on foot** for Blinds 16 & 17.
- To prevent disturbance of hunters in Blinds 16 & 17.
- Max **4 persons per blind**.
- 2 **mobility‑impaired blinds** via **quota hunts** (details at KY F&W link).

**LAKE BARKLEY WMA (Trigg, Lyon & Livingston)**

- **Refuge areas – closed to all hunting, fishing, boating, molesting of listed species**:
  - (a) West of main river channel from **river mile 51 (Hayes Landing Light)** to **TVA power lines at river mile 55.5**, **Nov. 1 – Feb. 15**.
  - (b) West of a line from north point of **mouth of Fulton Bay** to south point of **mouth of Honker Bay**, **Nov. 1 – Mar. 15**.

- **Duck Island**:
  - Boating allowed, but **hunting prohibited on and within 200 yards**.
  - Applies **Oct. 15 – Mar. 15**.

- **Permanent blind location rules (two defined areas)**:
  - Must remain within **10 yards** of assigned **numbered blind marker**.
  - Area (a): Mouth of **Donaldson Creek** → south along east side of old Cumberland River channel (buoys) → point due west of **Linton** boat ramp → east to Linton ramp → north along east shore back to Donaldson Creek.
  - Area (b): **Pryor’s Creek Light** → south along west shore of Barkley Lake to boat ramp at end of **LBL Road 204** → to west side of Cumberland River channel (buoys) → river mile **73.5** → north along west side of channel (buoys) to Pryor’s Creek Light.
- All other blinds within this described area must be **temporary**.

**LAND BETWEEN THE LAKES (Trigg & Lyon)**

- The following on **Kentucky Lake** closed to **all activity**:
  - **Long Creek Pond**.
  - Eastern **⅓ of Smith Bay**.
  - Eastern **⅔ of Duncan Bay**.
  - Closure period: **Nov. 1 – Mar. 15** (marked at south corner).
- **Environmental Education Center** and **Energy Lake**:
  - **Closed to all waterfowl hunting**.
- **LBL permit required**.
- **No waterfowl hunting on inland areas during quota gun deer hunts**.
- **Permanent blinds prohibited**:
  - On inland areas.
  - Along Kentucky Lake shoreline area.
- **Decoys and blinds must be removed daily** (end of each hunting day).
