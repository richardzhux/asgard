### License Year & General Rules

- **License year:** March 1 – last day of February.
- **New license required every year** except:
  - 3‑year fishing license
  - Lifetime Sportsman’s License
- **1-day / 7-day licenses**: only valid for the exact period shown.
- **Youth under 12 (resident & nonresident):**
  - Do **not** need licenses or permits  
  - **Exception:** still must buy **elk hunt drawing application** if applying.

---

### Major License Types (overview)

- **Annual Fishing:** Resident $23 / Nonresident $55  
- **1-Day Fishing:** Resident $7 / Nonresident $15  
- **3-Year Fishing (online only):** Resident $55  
- **Annual Hunting:** Resident $27 / Nonresident $160  
- **Short-term Hunting Licenses (nonresident):**
  - 1‑Day: $25 (not valid for deer, elk, turkey, bear)
  - 7‑Day: $65 (not valid for deer, elk, turkey, bear)
- **Annual Youth Hunting (12‑15):** Resident $6 / Nonresident $10  

---

### Combination & Sportsman’s Licenses

- **Annual Combination Hunting/Fishing:** Resident $42  
- **Sportsman’s License* (Resident $95)**  
  Includes:
  - Combination hunting/fishing
  - Statewide deer permit
  - Spring & fall turkey permits
  - KY Migratory Bird/Waterfowl Permit
  - Trout permit  
  **Does NOT include:** Peabody, LBL, Otter Creek permits; elk drawing & elk permits; bear permit; federal duck stamp; trapping license; **no extra deer permit.**

- **Senior/Disabled Sportsman’s (Resident $12)**  
  - Same components as standard Sportsman’s  
  - For residents: **65+** OR with qualifying permanent disability.

- **Senior Lifetime Sportsman’s (Resident $180)**  
  - Same components as Sportsman’s  
  - Must maintain **KY residency**  
  - For residents **65+**.

- **Youth Sportsman’s (12‑15, Resident $30)**  
  - Includes: hunting/fishing, statewide deer, spring & fall turkey, KY Migratory Bird/Waterfowl Permit.

### Selected Species Permits

**Deer**
- **Statewide Deer Permit (4 deer):** Resident $35 / Nonresident $235  
- **Youth Deer (12‑15, 4 deer):** Resident $10 / Nonresident $15  
- **Additional Deer Permit (2 deer):** $15 (resident & nonresident)

**Turkey**
- **Spring Turkey (statewide, 2 birds):** Resident $30 / Nonresident $110  
- **Fall Turkey (statewide, 2 birds):** Resident $30 / Nonresident $110  
- **Youth Turkey (12‑15, 1 turkey, spring OR fall):** Resident $10 / Nonresident $15  

**Migratory Birds & Waterfowl**
- **KY Migratory Bird/Waterfowl Permit:** $15 (for anyone required to have a hunting license who hunts migratory birds).
- **Federal Duck Stamp:** $25 (post offices or duckstamp.com).
- **Pheasant Quota Hunt (if drawn):** $25  
- **Sandhill Crane Permit:** $3 (resident & nonresident; quota; apply in Sept. online).

**Elk** (Quota Hunts)
- **Elk Drawing Application (adult):** $10  
- **Youth Elk Drawing (≤15):** $10  
- **Quota Bull Elk Firearm (if drawn):** Resident $100 / Nonresident $550  
- **Quota Cow Elk Firearm (if drawn):** Resident $60 / Nonresident $400  
- **Quota Either‑Sex Archery/Crossbow (if drawn):** Resident $100 / Nonresident $550  
- **Quota Youth Elk (12‑15, if drawn):** Resident $30 / Nonresident $200  
  - Youth drawn for other (non-youth) elk permits pay **full** permit cost.  
- **Out‑of‑Zone Elk Permit:** Resident $30 / Nonresident $400  

**Bear**
- **Bear Permit:** Resident $30 / Nonresident $250  
- **Youth Bear (12‑15):** Resident $10 / Nonresident $100  
- **Bear Chase Permit:** Resident $30 / Nonresident $50  
- **Youth Bear Chase (12‑15):** Resident $10 / Nonresident $15  
- **Combination Bear Permit (hunt + chase):** Resident $50

### Area‑Specific Fees

- **Ballard WMA 1‑Day Waterfowl Permit:** $15  
- **Peabody WMA User Permit:** $15  
- **Land Between The Lakes (LBL):**
  - Hunter Use Permit: $25
  - Hunt/Backcountry Camp: $50  
- **Otter Creek ORA:** Admission and special activity fees (horse, bike, shooting range) – check **fw.ky.gov** for current amounts.

- **Temporary Hunter Education Exemption (Hunter Apprentice):** Free, online only (fw.ky.gov).

- **Trapping licenses** (not included in Sportsman’s License):
