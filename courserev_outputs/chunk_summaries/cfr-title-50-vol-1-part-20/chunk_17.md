# Chunk 17 (units 13121-14020)

**Key Concepts / Sections**

**Subpart N — Special Procedures for Issuance of Annual Hunting Regulations (Part 20)**  
- Applies only to procedures for **annual regulations** on **seasons, bag limits, and other requirements** for hunting migratory birds (§20.151).  
- Does **not** apply to:
  - Part 21 (migratory bird permits)
  - Subparts A–J and L–M of Part 20.

1. **§20.152 Definitions (Subpart N context)**
   - **Flyway Council**: The Atlantic, Mississippi, Central, or Pacific Flyway Council.
   - **Regulations Committee**: Migratory Bird Regulations Committee of the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS).
   - **Significant (communication/info/data)**:
     - Related to the **merits of the regulation**; and  
     - Received/used/transmitted by a Department official involved, or reasonably expected to be involved, in the **decisional process** on the regulation.

2. **§20.153 Regulations Committee Procedures**
   - **Notice of meetings**:
     - Published in the **Federal Register** at least **2 weeks before** any meeting attended by persons outside the Department.
     - Must state time, place, general subjects, and **extent of public involvement**.
   - **Public observation and written comment**:
     - Such meetings are **open for public observation**.
     - Public may submit **written comments**.
   - **Public participation (oral)**:
     - For most noticed meetings, the public may give **oral statements** if they follow rules in the notice.
     - **Exception**: Mid‑summer meetings in Washington, DC (held with the public hearing on waterfowl/late season frameworks) do **not** allow this same oral participation mechanism.
   - **Minutes**:
     - Minutes must be made for each noticed meeting.

3. **§20.154 Flyway Councils**
   - **Notice of meetings**:
     - If any **Department official** will attend, notice will be published in the **Federal Register**:
       - At least **2 weeks before**, or  
       - As soon as practicable after the Department learns of the meeting.
     - Must state time, place, and general subject(s).
   - Paragraph (b) reserved (no current text).

4. **§20.155 Public File**
   - **Establishment**:
     - A **public file** is created for each rulemaking covered by Subpart N.
   - **Contents** (except information exempt under 5 U.S.C. 552 / FOIA):
     1. **Minutes** of Regulations Committee meetings (§20.153(d)).
     2. All **written comments** and other **significant written communications** occurring **after** the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM).
     3. **Summaries** (with source identified) of any **significant oral communications** after the NPRM.
     4. Copies of or references to any other **significant data or information**.

**§21.1 Purpose of Regulations (Part 21)**

- **Supplements Part 13**:
  - Adds **specific permit rules** for migratory birds to the general permit rules in **Part 13** of the same subchapter.
- Covers permits for:
  - **Taking**, **possession**, **transportation**, **sale**, **purchase**, **barter**, **importation**, **exportation**, and **banding or marking** of migratory birds.
- Provides certain **exceptions** to permit requirements for:
  - **Public institutions**
  - **Scientific institutions**
  - **Educational institutions**
- Establishes **depredation orders**:
  - These are limited exceptions to the **Migratory Bird Treaty Act (MBTA)** to allow control of birds causing damage (depredation).
